Be Thou My Vision

Verse 1a:
Be Thou my vision
O Lord of my heart
Naught be all else to me
Save that Thou art

Verse 1b:
Thou my best thought 
By day or by night
Waking or sleeping 
Thy presence my light

Verse 2a:
Be Thou my wisdom 
And Thou my true word
I ever with Thee and 
Thou with me, Lord

Verse 2b:
Thou my great Father
And I, Thy true son
Thou in me dwelling 
And I with Thee one

Verse 3a:
Riches I heed not 
Nor man's empty praise
Thou mine inheritance 
Now and always

Verse 3b:
Thou and Thou only 
First in my heart
High King of heaven 
My treasure Thou are

Verse 4a:
High King of heaven 
My victory won
May I reach heaven's joys
O bright heaven's Sun

Verse 4b:
Heart of my own heart 
Whatever befall
Still be my vision 
O Ruler of all
